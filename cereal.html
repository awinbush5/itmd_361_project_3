My Favorite Cereals

Golden Grahams

Golden Graham is having bite size graham crackers in your mouth. It is lightly sweet but if you do not eat it quickly it will get soggy.

Golden Grahams is one of the healthier cereals on the market

Favorite things about Golden Graham:

  • Not very sweet
  • Has a cinnamon flavor
  • Does not break apart

Frosted Flakes

Frosted Flakes is a classic cereal in itself and has a crunch that last for a while when it has milk in it

The edges of the flakes can sometimes be sharp

Favorite things about Frosted Flakes:

  • Crunch
  • Milk that is left over has a sweet taste to it

Honey Nut Cheerios

This cereal is something your family probably grew up on including your parents and grandparents

It stays very crunchy and probably out of the three will get soggy the last and least bit

Favorite things about Honey Nut Cheerios:

  • Taste
  • Size of cereal
  • Has a hint of sweetness
